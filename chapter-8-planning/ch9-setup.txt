CHAPTER 9 — FIRST OTHER WORLD

The party arrives on another world for the first time.

SETTING:
- The world is in the grip of deep winter.
- The party arrives in a town or village under siege from monsters.

LANGUAGE:
- Locals speak the same common tongue. Strange accents but understandable.
- The Estarans spread the common tongue throughout the cosmos.
- Fey languages, however, are completely undecipherable here.
  (The Estarans and the Fey don't get along — Fey languages didn't travel.)

LOCAL SITUATION:
- The Estarans were here and left about a year ago.
- Since then, the town has been under attack by a dragon and local monsters.
- The townfolk will assume the party were sent by the Estarans.

TORMA AND GULLUX:
- The party will see Torma the Unforgiving again.
- Possibly Gullux in another form — maybe a true dragon.
- This time they'll have to fight them directly.

ESTARAN ARTIFACTS:
- There are abandoned Estaran artifacts and technology to explore, learn from,
  and pillage.
