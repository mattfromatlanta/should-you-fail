CHAPTER 8 — DEPARTURE AND SETUP

By the end of Chapter 7, the party will have connected with Yrk and Felicity.
They've brought a prototype Yrk calls the Radiant Vessel. It allows the party to
follow the rifts left behind by regular use of a Radiant Projector.
Best guess destination: Metgal.

The party must leave Aris regardless of the state of things after the riot.
It's okay for the chapter to feel unresolved. There should be some amount of
saying goodbye before they set out.

The party does not know:
- where they are going exactly
- how they will accomplish what they need to
- whether they will be able to return

WHO CAN COME:
Only Cetyri can go with the party from here.
No Feywild fey can safely enter Metgal.
Xilo and Yrk will accompany the party but must stop at the border.

NOTE: As chapters continue and Cetyri grows more powerful through merging,
avoid scenarios where their dragon friend solves everything for them.
