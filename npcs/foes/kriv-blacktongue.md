# Kriv the Blacktongue

## Basic Information
**Race:** Black Dragon (Adult or Ancient)
**Role:** Greatest dragon of the Drekklands
**Status:** Deceased (found dead in Chapter 4)
**Territory:** Eastern Drekklands, Moldrum region north of the bay

## Description

The greatest and most powerful black dragon in the Drekklands. His territory dominated the eastern region, and he was feared by all who knew of him including the Bullywugs of Gulpfen Brood.

## Reputation

The party initially planned to avoid Kriv's territory entirely, traveling southwest through the Drekklands. However, they realized they needed to head east to find Yrk the Trickster, forcing them to cautiously enter the dragon's domain.

## Death

When the party arrived at Kriv's lair, they found the dragon already slain. Shortly after, they were attacked by Yrk the Trickster, suggesting Yrk was responsible for killing Kriv—a demonstration of the ancient goblin's formidable power.

## Significance

Kriv's death at Yrk's hands establishes the Trickster's capabilities. A goblin who can slay the greatest dragon in a region is not to be underestimated, foreshadowing the party's own defeat in their confrontation with Yrk.
