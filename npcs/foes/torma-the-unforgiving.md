# Torma the Unforgiving

## Basic Information
**Race:** Adult White Dragon
**Role:** Mother of Gullux and Bronik
**Status:** Active threat
**First Appearance:** Chapter 2 (mentioned in Chapter 1)

## Description

An adult white dragon whose eggs were stolen by her son Gullux. Described as unforgiving, living up to her epithet.

## Background

Torma is the mother of both Gullux and Bronik. Gullux betrayed her by stealing dragon eggs from her clutch, an act deserving of "a slow, painful death" in Bronik's assessment. The stolen treasure that Gullux took with him included items from her hoard: adamantine armor and a flaming sword that once belonged to Garrik the Bold, who challenged Torma and died.

## Current Situation

Torma seeks her stolen eggs and has sent Bronik to find them. She considers Gullux a traitor. Any who stand in the way of her search are doomed to face her wrath. She attacks at the end of Chapter 2.

## Death

Torma is killed by Erol and Lamat at the end of Chapter 2.

## Relationships

- **Gullux:** Traitorous son who stole from her
- **Bronik:** Loyal son serving as her agent
- **Garrik the Bold:** Challenger who was defeated and killed

## Note

Gullux is described as Torma's son in Chapter 2 materials, establishing that she is his mother, not merely the source of the stolen eggs.
