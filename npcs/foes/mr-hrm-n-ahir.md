# Mr'Hrm'N'Ahir, Aboleth Lord

## Basic Information
**Race:** Aboleth
**Role:** Master of Kua-Toa village in the Underwet
**Status:** Defeated (Chapter 4)
**First Appearance:** Chapter 4

## Description

An Aboleth lord who dominated the minds of Kua-Toa and enslaved Bullywugs in the underground river system known as the Underwet.

## Significant Action

When the party encountered Mr'Hrm'N'Ahir, the Aboleth attempted to establish "how many moments" this world has existed. This cryptic question represents one of the first direct hints the party receives that there have been worlds before the current Material Plane.

## Context

This question gains significance when combined with knowledge from the Testament of Embers, which reveals the Estara have annihilated the Material Plane 54 times to quell rebellions and defeat dragon singularities. The Aboleth's ancient knowledge suggests awareness of this cycle.

## Fate

Defeated by the party in combat. The Bullywug thralls (including members of the Gulpfen Brood) were freed from its mental domination.
