# Ooli Forgegarden

## Basic Information
**Race:** Dwarf
**Role:** City Counselor of Oechenfor
**Relationship:** Involved with Nirsa Hoegartner

## Description

Ooli Forgegarden serves as a counselor in Oechenfor's city government. He is involved in a relationship with Nirsa Hoegartner, a socialite who was previously married to fellow counselor Taram Thristle.
