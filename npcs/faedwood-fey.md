# Faedwood Fey

## Overview

The Fey of the Faedwood have traditionally been allies of Oechenfor but have become hostile due to Dragonborn hunting them for sport.

## Named Characters (Chapter 1, Encounter 02)

### Faerie Borrowers
- **Nightfeather**
- **Pineseed**

### Sprite Warriors
- **Thorna**
- **Whindwhip**
- **Lintee**
- **Snell**

### Pixie Warrior
- **Elmleaf:** Captured by Dragonborn and held at The Nest. Speaks Draconic and provides valuable intelligence if rescued. Willing to rally Fey forces against the invaders if given the chance.

## Behavior

The Fey are angry because their territory has been invaded by Dragonborn who have captured and eaten some of them. They initially blame townfolk for either causing or attracting the invaders. They can be convinced to share information and potentially ally with the party if shown sympathy.

Three faeries were captured by the invaders. Two were found dead and disfigured. Elmleaf is the third.

## Disposition

Start with adversarial attitude but can become allies. If helped, they will keep the Dragonborn confined to the Upland Stair and prevent them from ranging south into the Faedwood again.
