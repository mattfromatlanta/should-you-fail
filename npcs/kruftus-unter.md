# Kruftus Unter

## Basic Information
**Race:** Human (Werebear)
**Role:** Mayor of Lome
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 07

## Description

Kruftus is a tall, broad, muscular human with intense eyes and thick, dark hair. He stands 6'4" and weighs approximately 300 lbs. He has coarse fur-like hair, long fingernails that always appear dirty, and reacts strangely to strong smells—all subtle signs of his lycanthropy.

## Background

Kruftus was one of the founders of Lome five years ago, alongside Erol, Lamat, F'zuga, and others. As a powerful fighter, he led a disastrous attack on the Dragonborn invaders in which a dozen townspeople were killed. Since then, he has organized the defense of Lome with a confidence problem stemming from those losses. His main concern is keeping the people of Lome safe. His lycanthropy is known to a few but considered a private matter not discussed openly.

## Personality

Preoccupied, tired, brave. He is deeply worried about getting more people killed but knows action must be taken. The townspeople mostly believe in him, though confidence has wavered after the failed attack.

## Characteristics

- DC 15 Insight reveals something feral in his nature
- DC 20 Nature check suggests he could be a lycanthrope
- Will support a good plan if the party takes the lead
- Can provide 7 armed citizens, F'zuga, and himself for a counterattack
