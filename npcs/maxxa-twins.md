# Maxxa'Valien and Maxxa'Volien

## Basic Information
**Race:** Beholders
**Role:** Rival territorial lords of the Underdark canyon
**Status:** Active (Maxxa'Valien transformed by Milicent)
**First Appearance:** Chapter 2, Encounter 04

## Origin

Born when an unknown god's nightmare split in two. They are identical twins with one critical difference that defines their relationship.

## Maxxa'Valien

Describes himself as "perfect and majestic." Pale green skin with mottled ochre leopard spots. All eye stalks are a pleasing sunset orange, including the central eye. Lives in a fortress at one end of the underground canyon.

## Maxxa'Volien

Described by his twin as "absolutely unpleasant to look upon." Pale green skin with mottled ochre leopard spots. Central eye is deep violet. All eye stalks are sunset orange except the 8th, which is slightly darker—almost orange-yellow. Lives in an identical fortress at the opposite end of the canyon from Maxxa'Valien.

## The Rivalry

The twins have been at odds for an age but established a delicate contract splitting the canyon evenly. They recently achieved balance when deep dragons moved into the neutral zone between their territories, threatening the arrangement. The dragons took 3/64ths more territory from Maxxa'Valien than from Maxxa'Volien, which Maxxa'Valien finds "inexcusable."

Both are bound by their contract not to enter the neutral zone themselves, forcing them to rely on outside agents.

## Personality

- Arrogant and theatrical
- Obsessed with precision and fairness in territorial division
- Quick to threaten but pragmatic when they need help
- View surface creatures as "silly creatures from Outer Space"

## Fate

Maxxa'Valien is later captured by Milicent's servants and transformed into a monstrous test for the party. He first appears as a Zombie Beholder, then erupts as an Eye Shadow when that form is defeated.
