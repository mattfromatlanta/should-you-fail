# Gord Lankmangled

## Basic Information
**Race:** Dwarf
**Role:** Merchant of Arms (imprisoned)
**Business:** Owner of Lankmangled Arms
**Status:** Incarcerated in Oechenfor's prison

## Description

Gord Lankmangled is a dwarf merchant who owned Lankmangled Arms and was a direct competitor for Taram Thristle's seat on the city council.

## Criminal Activity

Cormin discovered that Gord kept a small group of artisans functionally under lock and key to make weapons for him—a deeply illegal form of indentured servitude. Cormin exposed the plot, leading to Gord's arrest.

## Current Status

Gord now sits in Oechenfor's prison and bears Cormin no small amount of ill will for his role in the exposure and arrest.
