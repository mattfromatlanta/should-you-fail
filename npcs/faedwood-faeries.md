# Faedwood Faeries

A group of faeries living in the Faedwood.

## Nightfeather

A tall, elegant faerie of dusk and shadow. Her wings resemble those of a raven brushed with starlight. Watchful, perceptive, and cautious in speech.

## Pineseed

Sturdy and warm-spirited, with sap-sticky fingers and evergreen eyes. Acts older than they appear. Deeply connected to the Faedwood's ancient rhythms and lore. Frequently murmurs old forest songs.
