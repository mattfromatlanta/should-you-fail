# Melbara, Queen Librarian

## Basic Information
**Race:** Eladrin
**Role:** Queen Librarian, Moon Druid
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 3
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

A powerful Moon Druid who has guarded Naeliste's most ancient knowledge for over three centuries. Grandmother of Aelira and mother-in-law of Caera. Fierce guardian of knowledge with a shapeshifter's rare control and ferocity.

## Background

Melbara has served as Naeliste's Queen Librarian for over three centuries. Despite her regal bearing, she is not of royal blood—"Queen" is an honorific granted for her stewardship of the city's collection of antiquities, some of which predate even the current city itself.

As a Druid of the Moon, Melbara is a shapeshifter of rare control and ferocity. She has long believed in the necessity of strong forms and swift action, and once served as a guardian of the Faedwood's outer reaches before dedicating herself to knowledge.

She now serves as curator of the Library of Rhymes and Antiquities in Naeliste. She possesses rare knowledge including the Testament of Embers, an ancient Estaran epic.

## Personality

Knowledgeable about ancient history, particularly regarding Milicent and the Fey. She is discerning but has judged Aelira harshly in the past. She later shares a private letter from Caera as an expression of trust and quiet remorse, hoping to repair that distance.

## Knowledge

- Ancient history of Milicent and the Rebel Fey
- The Testament of Embers (describing Varkhasia the red Unary Great Wyrm)
- May not volunteer information about the Rebel Fey but will acknowledge it when asked
- Recognizes Bo'Lug's riddle as connected to the Rebel Fey

## Current Role

Maintains Melbara's private collection in the Library of Rhymes and Antiquities. Provides guidance to Aelira during research and shares family history.
