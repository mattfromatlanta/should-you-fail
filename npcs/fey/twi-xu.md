# Twi'Xu, Animal Lord

## Basic Information
**Race:** Fey (Animal Lord)
**Role:** Lieutenant of Bluebirds, Vassal to Xilo
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 6a
**Location:** Feywild Wasteland (Rinick's Diner)

## Description

A powerful Fey creature who serves as one of Xilo's closest advisors and inner council. As an Animal Lord, Twi'Xu commands authority over beasts in the Feywild wasteland. He is disciplined, patient, and understands the deeper stakes of the conflict with the Estarans—he recognizes the debt all Rebel Fey owe to Milicent for the role she played in driving them away.

## Background

Twi'Xu was stationed at Rinick's Diner on Xilo's orders to monitor Egedzoo's movements. The adult blue dragon had been consolidating power among the hobgoblin bandit factions in the wasteland, and Xilo needed intelligence before deciding how to respond. Twi'Xu could not leave his post—doing so would have given Egedzoo unchecked freedom to operate.

When the party arrived at Rinick's, they stumbled into a tense standoff: Twi'Xu and Egedzoo watching each other in dangerous silence, neither willing to break the neutral-ground truce. After Egedzoo departed, Twi'Xu engaged the party and learned they were working with Milicent. This immediately elevated their status in his eyes.

## Relationship with the Party

Twi'Xu was instrumental in arranging the party's meeting with Xilo. He confirmed that Cetyri and Bronik had passed through heading to Qu, and vouched for the party to Xilo's inner circle. He could not accompany them himself, but his introduction made Xilo's eventual meeting possible.

## Personality

Calm, watchful, and loyal. Twi'Xu does not act impulsively—his surveillance of Egedzoo lasted as long as it needed to without escalating. He respects power and purpose in equal measure, and was quickly convinced that the party possessed both. He understands the historical weight of Milicent's sacrifice in the First Rebellion.

## Current Role

Status following the Egedzoo assault is unknown. He remains one of Xilo's trusted lieutenants in the wasteland.
