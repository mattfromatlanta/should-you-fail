# Hootini

## Basic Information
**Race:** Moon Fey (Owl)
**Role:** Aelira's fey companion, former servant of Milicent
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1 (Familiar + Summoned Fey), Chapter 6 (True Form, encountered in the Feywild)

## Description

A fey owl companion to Aelira. One of the Moon Fey who once served Milicent before she became the Shadowdame.

## Background

Hootini served Milicent during her time as a scholar in the Feywild, when she operated her observatory at Ozevriel's memorial tower. He has deep knowledge of her transformation and research on the Estarans.

## Role in Chapter 6

Found the party at Ozevriel's memorial and guided them through Milicent's research documents. Provided context about her transformation from scholar to Shadowdame. Sensed Xilo's arrival and facilitated the meeting.

## Current Status (End of Chapter 6)

Officially part of Fey delegation traveling to Aris. Cannot leave this world permanently but can be summoned once by Aelira when party departs for other realities. Relationship with party: Trusted advisor and guide, particularly connected to Aelira.
