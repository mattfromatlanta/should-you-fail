# Th'Rubba M'Ukid, Minister of Marshculture

## Basic Information
**Race:** Bullywug
**Role:** Minister of Marshculture
**Status:** Active
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

Draped in woven lily-stem robes and smelling faintly of rich pondwater, Th'Rubba greets strangers with a grin and a half-sung proverb. Her job is to keep the marsh and the people in balance—and she takes both personally.
