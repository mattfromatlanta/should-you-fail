# Ha'Lopp Fernwater, Captain of the Guard

## Basic Information
**Race:** Bullywug
**Role:** Captain of the Guard
**Status:** Active
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

Broad-shouldered and stone-still, Ha'Lopp runs Naeliste's patrols with calm authority and knows how to track a threat through reeds or fog. His eyes miss nothing, and he doesn't waste words on warnings.
