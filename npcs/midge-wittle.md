# Midge Wittle

## Basic Information
**Race:** Rock Gnome
**Role:** Proprietor of The Wary Hippogriff
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 07

## Description

A Rock Gnome entrepreneur running Lome's only inn and common house.

## Background

Midge settled in Lome about a year ago hoping to start a mining operation but gained no traction. He switched to get-rich-quick schemes while running The Wary Hippogriff.

## Personality

Friendly, aloof, interested in profit. Very interested in finding out who the party are and how he can use them for gain. The only person in town willing to drop hints to strangers about the mayor's lycanthropy, mostly out of boredom.

## Current Status

The Wary Hippogriff has been turned into a refuge for farmers whose homes were destroyed by the invaders. Midge is unhappy about giving away lodgings but accepts it as necessary. He approaches the party about leading a fight and seeks potential business partners.
