# Ahkkathassys

## Basic Information
**Race:** Lizardfolk (Young Adult Male)
**Role:** Oechenfor Founder, Traveling Bard
**Origin:** The Drekklands

## Description

Ahkkathassys is a traveling bard from the Drekklands and associate of Adrum Zannis. He was recruited to come to Oechenfor at the suggestion of Aldrin Stoneward, becoming one of the six founders of the city. He still resides in Oechenfor.
