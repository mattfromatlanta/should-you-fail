# Adrum Zannis

## Basic Information
**Race:** Svirfneblin (Deep Gnome) Female, Aged
**Role:** Oechenfor Founder
**Personality:** Clever, Visionary, Ambitious

## Description

Adrum Zannis is a veteran of both the Estaran campaigns and the Rebellion. As one of the six founders of Oechenfor, she brought both military experience and strategic vision to the city's establishment. She recruited Ahkkathassys to come to Oechenfor at the suggestion of Aldrin Stoneward.

## Adrum's Vault

Adrum operates a subterranean archive and secure storage facility in Oechenfor. The Vault serves as a records hall, magical repository, and neutral ground for high-value exchanges. Entry requires recommendation or sponsorship by one of the city's Founders. Adrum's descendants help maintain the facility.
