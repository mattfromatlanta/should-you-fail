# Caera

## Basic Information
**Race:** Eladrin
**Role:** Druid of the Land, Mother of Aelira
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 2, Encounter 01
**Location:** Naeliste
**Relationship:** Daughter-in-law of Melbara

## Description

Caera is a Druid of the Land from Naeliste, drawing power from quiet mysteries of the ancient woods. She is more attuned to dreams, omens, and the unspoken language of flora and fauna than she is to politics or traditions. Her preferred wild shape is a Night Heron—mysterious and elegant. The heron allows Caera to glide silently across forested wetlands and observe from the shadows.

## Background

Caera fought in the Rebellion alongside her partner Thorn, who is Aelira's father. They both fought to make a world their daughter could walk through without fear. After the Rebellion ended 150 years ago, Caera returned to raise Aelira. Since Thorn vanished years ago, she has withdrawn from much of public life, though she remains a quiet presence among Naeliste's scholars and civic leaders.

She pledged to help Lome recover but was forced to return to Naeliste when it became known that Milicent has begun abducting mortals for the Dread Legion.

Caera has known Erol for many years and deeply respects her. She describes Erol as "as sure of herself as your grandmother. Indomitable." She confirms that Erol is female.
