# Brother Alistair Thornfield

## Basic Information
**Race:** Unknown
**Role:** Cleric of Oechenfor
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 01

## Description

A cleric serving in Oechenfor.

## Role in Story

If the party is reluctant to volunteer for Aldrin's mission, Brother Alistair approaches them privately. He reveals that Aldrin knows who they are and believes they have the capability to help the town. He offers 50 gp to convince them.

If the party refuses entirely, Brother Alistair eventually leads an expedition that clears out the invaders and returns things to normal, though this results in negative consequences for the town and region.
