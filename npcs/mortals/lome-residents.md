# Lome Residents

## Monks of the Monastery

Led by F'zuga, the monastery consists of a dozen orc monks who train for defense and craft tools.

Named monks: Samff't, R'gun, Bulruch, Haff't, Orad, Mulg'x

## Orc Homesteaders

Families who settled in the Uplands around Lome:
- Barvig and Valoot
- Jamella and Th'khora
- Fad'xoon and Lut

## Human Homesteaders

Families who settled in the Uplands around Lome:
- Vassa and Mindy Blacktooth
- Carem and Ninda Tootall
- George the Hacker

## Tradesfolk

- **Gaddish:** Human hunter, tanner, and furrier
- **Smoglin:** Orc blacksmith and carpenter
- **Vogel:** Orc monk, brewer, and alchemist
- **Morton Steamrider:** Human trader

## Population

Lome has less than 200 residents in the town proper. Homesteaders in the surrounding area bring the regional population higher. The town is a mix of orcs and humans. Orcs tend to be more circumspect and distrustful. Both groups value privacy and minding one's own business.

## Town Defense

Kruftus can rally 7 sturdy citizens armed with spears and hide armor for a counterattack if convinced. Many residents were killed in an earlier failed assault on the Dragonborn hideout.
