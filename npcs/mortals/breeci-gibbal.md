# Breeci Gibbal

## Basic Information
**Race:** Svirfneblin (Deep Gnome)
**Role:** Wizard/Apothecary
**Reputation:** Maybe-slightly-shady
**Connection:** Seasonal employer of Peno

## Description

Breeci Gibbal is a Svirfneblin wizard and apothecary operating in Oechenfor. He has a reputation for being maybe-slightly-shady. He seasonally employs Peno the Goliath.
