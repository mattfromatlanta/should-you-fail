# F'zuga

## Basic Information
**Race:** Orc
**Role:** Ascetic, Leader of Lome Monastery
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 07

## Description

An orc monk dressed in simple, free-flowing robes with warrior reflexes. Commands respect and leads through quiet wisdom.

## Background

F'zuga is Keeper of the Orchard Path Monastery in Lome, which was founded and patronized by Lamat. He was mentored by Lamat when they founded Lome five years ago. Under his leadership, a dozen orc monks train for defense of the town and lead ceremonies honoring service. The monks also craft tools for farmers.

## Philosophy

A quiet, disciplined presence who believes strong roots and simple comforts make a community last. He advises Kruftus and Midge on town matters, focusing on productive farms, steady labor, and lives lived in quiet seclusion.

## Personality

Wise, quiet, grim. Deeply concerned about Lamat's disappearance and eager for any news about his mentor.

## Current Status

Leading monastery defense efforts. Will join a counterattack against the invaders if a solid plan is presented. Wants to fight but knows his monks cannot succeed alone. Approaches the party to learn what they know about Lamat.
