# Taram Thristle

## Basic Information
**Role:** City Counselor of Oechenfor
**Age:** Young
**Status:** Divorced (formerly married to Nirsa)

## Description

Taram Thristle is a young city councilman of some note in Oechenfor. He's an old friend of Cormin's from school days.

## Personal History

Taram enlisted Cormin's help in discovering whether his wife Nirsa (human) was having an affair. Cormin's investigation revealed that she was indeed involved with Gord Lankmangled. The marriage ended, and Nirsa reverted to her maiden name, becoming Nirsa Hoegartner.
