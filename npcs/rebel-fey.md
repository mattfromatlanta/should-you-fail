# The Rebel Fey

## Overview

The Rebel Fey are the Archfey and Beast Lords of the Feywild who faced the Estara in the First War. They remember the Material World from before the Estara's arrival and still fear their return.

## The Seven Original Lords

### Itrina
Watches from far beyond the frost with a feathered brow and gaze that tears through lies. He holds the names the waking world has lost and speaks in dreams to hearts too tired for cries. He sees what was, what is, and what should not be.

### Carris
Crowned in antlers dark with moss and thunder's stain. He held the lawless world within his will and kept the borders no one else could name. His oaths never broke or waned. When forest weeps, his hoofbeats are still heard.

### Xilo, Lord of Robins
A riddle's end, a feather's flick, a grin—joy beneath the thorn. He spoke in verse and taught the lost to win with riddles bright and laughter sharply born. Where he passed, shadows thinned and died, though sorrow nests where robins used to fly.

### Ozevriel (Deceased)
The shield of flame, the calm before the wrath. He stood alone and turned the Estara from their chosen path, allowing the other six to escape. His antlers broke; he wept but did not ask. Every age since has held the cost.

### Thessa
Walks slow with petals red as sin, where broken hearts grow gardens in the dusk. She binds the bloom to all that bleeds within and lifts the shattered mask from grief's cold husk. She does not judge the ruin or the rain but crowns the pain that dared to rise again.

### Varuun
Wears two curving horns—one points to fate, the other back to shame. He guards the places worlds divide and speaks rarely though he knows all names. He holds the choice—the turn, the thread, the flame. Where roads are lost, his silence walks beside you.

### Milicent (Fallen)
Moon-eyed, voice of stars, beauty bent to fire. She led them forth when shadows learned to crawl—when hope and wrath walked close upon the mire. But power broke what sorrow first inspired. She turned from light and gave her name away. None now speak the price she chose to pay.

## Current Status

Five remain after Ozevriel's sacrifice and Milicent's fall. They dwell far from Milicent in the deep Feywild. Their relationship to her is complex—bound by old loyalty but opposed to her current path. They want to see her stopped.

## Knowledge

- Possess knowledge of the Shadow Projector
- Understand Cetyri's potential as a Unary Great Wyrm
- Know that Unary Great Wyrms are something the Estara fear
- Have worked out how one might come to be
- Don't know that this has been Milicent's intent with Cetyri

## Characteristics

- Not kind, not safe
- Morally ambiguous with ancient knowledge
- Will not tell the party what they want, only what the Rebels need
- Not allies, but want Milicent stopped
- Demanding and rarely ask nicely
- Reaching them is difficult and dangerous

## Who Can Guide the Party to Them

- **Melbara:** Knows ancient history, won't volunteer but will acknowledge when asked
- **Erol:** Having served Milicent, understands the risks; may suggest directly
- **Eigitch:** Knows forgotten stories, may point the way indirectly
- **Bo'Lug Longtongue:** Can sense when the path leads to them, speaks in riddles

## Bo'Lug's Riddle (Invitation)

"Swamp sings different when the old roots stir. Heard it last when the moon hid behind herself and the water forgot how to ripple. You want answers? Don't look for lanterns on the path—follow the oldest frog songs back home. That's where the forgotten lords listen, still wet with sorrow. They don't come when called. But they'll know when you're coming."

## Purpose in Campaign

The Rebel Fey serve as a meaningful option when players are uncertain what to do next. Their remote presence, moral ambiguity, and ancient knowledge offer an open-ended path forward with both danger and discovery.
