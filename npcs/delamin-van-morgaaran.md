# Delamin Van Morgaaran

## Basic Information
**Role:** Head of Oechenfor offices of Van Morgaaran Trading
**Relationship:** Cormin's eldest brother

## Description

Delamin Van Morgaaran leads the Oechenfor branch of the powerful Van Morgaaran Trade Company. The Company operates with a wide margin of freedom, coordinating merchant traffic, warehouse contracts, and long-range shipments across the Allied Cities. Under Delamin's leadership, the Company has built roads, warehouses, river docks, and an efficient guild-run market system that drives much of Oechenfor's prosperity.

Some whisper that the Van Morgaarens are more powerful than the city's Counselors themselves.
