# Chevin the Silkstrand, Goblin Ne'er-do-well

## Basic Information
**Race:** Goblin
**Role:** Social connector, ne'er-do-well
**Status:** Active
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

Personal associate of Sylvaris. Chevin earned his moniker by casting social webs all over the city. He loves to make connections and tends to get involved in everything whether he should or not.

Sylvaris headed off to catch up with Chevin shortly after arriving in Naeliste, and hasn't been seen since.
