# Aldrin Stoneward

## Basic Information
**Race:** Human
**Role:** Chief Citizen and Founder of Oechenfor
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 01

## Description

Aldrin is a middle-aged human man with dark skin and a bald head like many veterans. Tall and regal with a seeming radiance, he carries the legendary longsword Valor on his back.

## Background

Aldrin was a veteran before the Rebellion and was gifted by the Estara with ageless, enduring life. He became an ally of Maelric Sturmwald and the Ancient Dragon Gallifax when the cities rose up against the Estara. He survived the burning of Metgal and founded Oechenfor alongside other heroes, including Erol the Faed.

## Personality

Wise, duty-bound, and concerned about the state of the realm. He carries himself with gravitas and appears troubled by recent events requiring the first Citizen Conference in years.

## Current Status

Departing Oechenfor for Aris to attend the Citizen Conference. Before leaving, he calls for volunteers to investigate the silence from Lome and the hostility in the Faedwood.
