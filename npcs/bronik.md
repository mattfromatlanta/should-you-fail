# Bronik

## Basic Information
**Race:** White Half-Dragon
**Role:** Son of Torma, Brother of Gullux
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 2, Encounter 02

## Description

A white half-dragon who rides a wyvern named Dirge. Unlike his brother Gullux, Bronik remained loyal to their mother Torma.

## Background

Bronik is the son of Torma the Unforgiving and brother to the traitorous Gullux. He has been searching for Gullux and his followers after Gullux betrayed their mother by stealing dragon eggs from her clutch. Gullux was always jealous and insecure, which led to his monstrous grafting.

## Mission

Torma sent Bronik to find the stolen treasure and learn the fate of the eggs. He seeks tribute to repay the stolen treasure and the audacity of taking it. When he encounters the party, he recognizes items from Torma's hoard—adamantine armor and a flaming sword once owned by Garrik the Bold.

## Personality

Direct and confrontational. Expects people to lie to him and tries to trick them into revealing the truth. Not bothered by the death of Gullux and his minions, viewing them as traitors. Will not forgive the destruction of Torma's eggs if he learns of it.

## Combat Approach

Attempts to fight the party but recognizes when outnumbered. The party captures him while he searches for his dead brother.

## Character Development

Bronik is captured by the party during Chapter 2. Over time, he comes to accept the party and is accepted by them in return. He becomes a trusted ally and companion.

## Journey with the Party

- Captured while searching for Gullux (who is already dead)
- Eventually accepts and is accepted by the party
- Leaves the Underdark with the party and Cetyri
- Travels with them to Naeliste in the Feywild
- Later flees to the Feywild with Cetyri when the dragon becomes worried about questions from Lomani Nae (this occurs while the party seeks the Trickster with Eigitch the Old and travels to meet Milicent)
- Accompanies party through Feywild adventures (Chapter 6)
- Stays out of combat but provides support to Cetyri

## Current Status (End of Chapter 6)

Returning to Material Plane with party and Cetyri. Continues as loyal friend to Cetyri, part of the extended group. Relationship with party: Trusted companion.

## Possessions

- Dirge (wyvern mount)
- 190gp pocket money
- Potion of Giant Strength (Hill)
