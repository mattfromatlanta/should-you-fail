# Gallifax

## Basic Information
**Race:** Ancient Bronze Dragon
**Role:** Aegis and Steward of Free People
**Territory:** Allied Cities region

## Description

Gallifax is an ancient bronze dragon who serves as protector and ally to the Allied Cities. His titles "Aegis and Steward of Free People" reflect his role as guardian of the mortal alliance.

## History

### Alliance with the Estara

For generations, Gallifax was a long-standing ally of the Estara during their rule over the Allied Cities. The ancient dragon supported their dominion and worked alongside them.

### The Turning Point

During the Rebellion approximately 80 years ago, when mortals rose up against Estaran oppression, Gallifax made a momentous decision. As the conflict reached Metgal and the Estara unleashed nightmare magic that burned the city, Gallifax witnessed their cruelty. Showing their true nature through such destruction, the Estara finally lost Gallifax's support.

The ancient dragon abandoned the Estara and joined the mortals' cause. This betrayal of his former allies proved to be a turning point in the war, helping the mortals achieve victory and banish the Estara.

## Significance

Gallifax's decision to switch sides during the Rebellion was instrumental in the mortals' success. An ancient dragon of his power and experience joining their cause provided both martial strength and symbolic legitimacy to the rebellion. His continued protection of the Allied Cities helps maintain their independence and security.
