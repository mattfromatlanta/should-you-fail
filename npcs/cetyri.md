# Cetyri, Gold Dragon Revenant

## Basic Information
**Race:** Gold Dragon (Revenant)
**Role:** Former protector, prisoner, now undead
**Status:** Blind and traumatized
**First Appearance:** Chapter 2, Encounter 05

## Background

Cetyri was once a protector of good people until he was captured by Milicent. She used him to train her Death Knights for a century. He died as her prisoner but returned as a revenant, driven by hunger for revenge.

## Current Condition

Cetyri is blind and doesn't know where he is. He assumes he is still in Milicent's dungeon. Learning he is elsewhere is news to him. He suspects he has died but cannot reconcile this with what he is experiencing. His body was apparently carried to the Underdark by water after being discarded.

## Future Significance

Cetyri is described as the "lost dragon" key to Milicent's plan in later story arcs. She seeks to use his instances across worlds to invoke the Gold Dragon Singularity, a final weapon against the Estarans. However, he must join the cause willingly.

## Restoration

Cetyri is blind "for the moment" and will require intervention from unknown powers to restore his sight.

## Personality

Traumatized by a century of captivity and torture. Confused about his current state. Driven by vengeance against Milicent.

## Journey with the Party

The party encounters Cetyri in the Underdark during Chapter 2. They leave the Underdark together, with Cetyri accompanied by both the party and Bronik (Gullux's brother, now the party's ally). The group travels to Naeliste in the Feywild.

## Flight to the Feywild

While the party is away seeking the Trickster with Eigitch the Old and eventually traveling to meet Milicent, Cetyri becomes worried about questions being asked by Lomani Nae. He flees deeper into the Feywild, taking Bronik with him. The party must later track them down and convince Cetyri to join their cause willingly—a difficult task given that Milicent, who tortured him for a century, is involved in the plan.
