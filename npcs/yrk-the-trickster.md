# Yrk the Trickster (Ar'gix)

## Basic Information
**Race:** Goblin
**Age:** Nearly 2000 years
**Role:** The Trickster, Co-creator of the Shadow Projector
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 3
**Location:** The Drekklands

## Description

An ancient goblin who has survived for nearly two millennia through cunning, misdirection, and refusal to be found.

## History

- Fought in the First War against the Estara as part of the mortal rebellion
- Survived by avoiding direct confrontation, using misdirection, sabotage, and retreat
- Was close to Milicent during the early rebellion; they collaborated for decades
- Helped build the Shadow Projector's core design ~400 years ago
- Did not realize it would be used to enslave mortals
- When Milicent began using it to create the Dread Legion, he broke ties and fled
- Has lived in isolation since, moving through remote and forgotten parts of the world

## Current Habits and Motives

- Avoids authority and attention; refuses to be found unless he chooses
- Distrusts all large-scale plans, alliances, and movements, including the Rebel Fey
- Does not consider himself hero or villain
- Values survival, autonomy, and subversion of power
- Maintains a network of odd magical constructs, dead drops, and layered illusions
- Uses stories, false trails, and rumors to protect himself
- Believes the only path to safety is through unpredictability

## Why He Stays in the Drekklands

- Geographically remote and magically unstable—hard to map, harder to follow
- Knows old routes, hidden thresholds, and buried places others avoid
- Locals consider him a myth and leave him alone
- If Milicent comes looking, he wants to be somewhere she can't move cleanly
- Keeps an eye on threats including Estaran return or shadow incursions

## Methods of Testing

Yrk observes and tests visitors through:
- **Familiar Echoes:** Small constructs (talking frogs, animated fungi) that repeat the party's words
- **Space Loops:** Manipulating unstable terrain to watch how people respond to confusion
- **False Omens:** Forged signs to see if visitors trust instinct, logic, or panic
- Various tricks and surreal encounters (reverse thefts, mock trials, echo duels)

## Knowledge

- Deepest knowledge of the Shadow Projector's structure and purpose
- Knows the concept of Radiant Projectors (original Estaran design)
- Has never seen a Radiant Projector but reckons he could build one for the right cause
- Understanding of Milicent's methods and motivations

## Relationship to Eigitch

Great-great-great-grandfather to Eigitch the Old. Only they remember Yrk's true name. Their reunion is marked by dry affection buried under minimal words.

## The Trickster's Blessing

Can provide protection from Milicent's gaze, making the party safe from her tracking on the Material Plane.
