# Gullux, the Iceflesh Grafter

## Basic Information
**Race:** White Dragonborn (Modified)
**Role:** Leader of Dragonborn Invaders
**Status:** Defeated (Chapter 1)
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 09

## Description

Gullux is a white half-dragon, son of Torma the Unforgiving, who has transformed himself into something monstrous through grafting. He has the size and strength of an ogre, with pieces of his body replaced by true dragon parts. The grafts are held together by stitched twine, leather bands, and metal staples. He radiates cold, hateful energy.

## Background

Gullux is the half-dragon son of Torma the Unforgiving and brother to Bronik. Corrupted by delusions of grandeur, he grafted dragon parts onto his already half-dragon form. He led a band of fifty White Dragonborn from the East Hills into the Uplands during winter. He betrayed his mother by stealing dragon eggs from her clutch. The theft and his boldness in claiming the eggs established his leadership among the clans. His brother Bronik describes him as always jealous and insecure.

## Leadership

Commanded his warriors through force of personality and martial prowess. His followers came down for sport and profit at his command. The crude dialect spoken by his clan was unfamiliar to local Draconic speakers.

## Defeat

The party confronted Gullux in a shadowy reflection space created by Milicent. After his defeat, all remaining Dragonborn in the hideout were found dead with no external wounds, killed by Milicent.
