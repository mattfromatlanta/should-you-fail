# Maelyn Heatherfields

## Basic Information
**Race:** Halfling
**Role:** Officer of Van Morgaaran Trading, Delamin's lieutenant

## Description

Maelyn Heatherfields serves as an officer in Van Morgaaran Trading and is Delamin Van Morgaaran's lieutenant. She is a true ally of Cormin, who once helped her by digging up dirt on a suitor who seemed too good to be true (and indeed was).
