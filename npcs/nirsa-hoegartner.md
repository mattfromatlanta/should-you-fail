# Nirsa Hoegartner

## Basic Information
**Race:** Human
**Role:** Socialite
**Former Name:** Nirsa Thristle (when married to Taram)

## Description

Nirsa Hoegartner is a socialite in Oechenfor. Formerly Nirsa Thristle when she was married to Taram Thristle, she was caught having an affair with Gord Lankmangled before he was arrested and jailed. She is now involved with Ooli Forgegarden, a dwarf counselor.
