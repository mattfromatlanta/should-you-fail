# Felicity Roo

## Basic Information
**Race:** Human
**Role:** Powerful Warlock of Milicent
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 4

## Description

Leader of the quickling team conducting kidnapping operations in Oechenfor for Milicent.

## Role in Chapter 5

Led the abduction of promising young citizens in Oechenfor. Used specialized star metal tools crafted (unwittingly) by Gord Lankenmangled. Set fire trap to lure party into shadow space, which ultimately led to the meeting with Milicent.

## Current Status (End of Chapter 6)

Traveling mortal world with Yrk, assisting with portable radiant projector and star model project. Will arrive in Aris a few days after the party. Relationship with party: Previous antagonist. Directly represents Milicent's interests in the mortal world.
