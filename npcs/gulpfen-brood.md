# Gulpfen Brood

## Overview

A Bullywug tribe that fell on hard times in the Drekklands and attempted to flee to the Faedwood. Only a few survivors reached Bullhome (the ruined keep at the lake). The tribe was split when the strongest members set out through the water passages while others waited at the keep. Those who went through the passages were captured by Kua-Toa and enslaved by the Aboleth Mr'Hrm'N'Ahir.

## Survivors at Bullhome

### Gu'Rup
**Role:** Leader of the keep survivors

Older Bullywug, always damp, wears a reed hat he never removes. Led the group that stayed to wait for word it would be safe to continue.

### Preep
**Role:** Lookout

Anxious and twitchy, keeps watch from the broken tower. Terrified of echoes. Always alert for danger.

### Tuckle
**Role:** Craftsman and gardener

Broad-faced and quietly competent. Tends a rooftop moss garden and makes tools out of scrap. Doesn't talk much but is highly capable of repairing tools and building simple structures with available materials.

### Nish-Nish
**Role:** Youngest member

The nosiest of the group. Carries a bundle of odd tokens—shiny shells, buttons, feathers—each one a clue to solve a mystery game she plays alone.

## Keep Management

The survivors maintained Bullhome's rain catchers and scavenged usable gear from the armory and kitchens. They repaired only what was absolutely necessary, believing too much change would draw dangerous attention. They protected the boundary with a troop of ogre zombies they raised for defense.

## Rescued Members

Vardo, Diniferous, Fub, and Malaki were among those who went through the water passages. They were captured by Kua-Toa, enslaved by the Aboleth, and eventually freed by the party.

## Knowledge

The Gulpfens know the state of the Drekklands well:
- The entire region has become home to black dragons
- The greatest is Kriv the Blacktongue, last known to reside in the east Moldrum north of the bay
- Great reptilian beasts roam the swamps
- Scattered troops of Lizardfolk and Svirfneblin hunt trespassers

## Reunion

The party successfully reunited the separated members of the Gulpfen Brood, bringing together those at Bullhome with those freed from the Aboleth's thralldom.
