# Milicent's Secret War

## Overview

Milicent, the Shadowdame and fallen Rebel Fey, is fighting a covert campaign against the Estara. She has been building the Dread Legion starting in frontier cities, particularly Oechenfor, while the Allied Cities remain unaware of the larger conflict.

## Milicent's History

- One of the seven original Rebel Fey who fought the Estara in the First War
- Was Erol the Faed's first patron
- Led the Rebel Fey before her fall from grace
- Collaborated with Yrk the Trickster for decades
- Built the Shadow Projector with Yrk ~400 years ago
- Turned from light and gave her name away
- Power broke what sorrow first inspired

## The Shadow Projector

A corrupted adaptation of ancient Estaran Radiant Projectors. Theorized by Milicent and built by Yrk.

### Function
- Housed in a mountain fortress in Milicent's realm in the Shadowfell
- Uses the Material Plane as a lens (the "Essential Knot")
- Projects concentrated darkness across realities
- Captures any creature that passes through the projection
- Draws captured beings into Milicent's fortress arena
- Gradually transforms captives into shadow beings, erasing their original selves
- Over time, prisoners become part of the Dread Legion

### Original Design: Radiant Projectors
Ancient Estaran devices that moved mortals between planes by casting a beam of energy that reflects between planes like a mirrored corridor. The mirrored place exists simultaneously in both realms, allowing transit. Mortals exposed are permanently affected—physically, magically, or psychically. Changes can be gifts or burdens, but all are altered. None remain in use or known to exist in the current world.

### Who Knows About It
- **Erol:** Knows a device exists but not details; left before its creation
- **The Rebel Fey:** Firsthand knowledge of function, purpose, and location
- **Yrk:** Built it with Milicent; deepest knowledge of structure and purpose
- **Milicent:** Creator and operator

## Current Operations

### In Oechenfor
- Quicklings are abducting townsfolk
- People are disappearing, city on brink of chaos

### In Aris
- Infiltrated and overrun with doppelgangers loyal to Milicent
- Goal is to weaken Allied Cities from within
- Milicent placed Maelric (Chief Citizen Exarch) into permanent dream to paralyze leadership
- Erol has traveled there to shield the city and Aldrin from the Shadow Projector
- Erol hopes to revive Maelric from his dream

## The Estaran Threat

### What Milicent Knows
Unknown to all but Milicent, the Estara are preparing a massive weapon—a cosmic-scale death machine to control their vast Empire. This is not the first such device. They have annihilated the entire Material Plane to quell rebellions and defeat dragon singularities 54 times.

### The Estaran Empire
- The Estara have invaded and conquered hundreds of worlds
- They keep worlds separate, making it impossible for mortals to detect the scale of subjugation
- Veterans' scattered and fragmented memories are a direct result of this isolation strategy
- Milicent knows the other worlds are out there, still under the Estara's thumb

## Milicent's Goal

She dreams of the Estara's final destruction. She knows time is running out. Her plan involves:
1. Building the Dread Legion through the Shadow Projector
2. Creating a Unary Great Wyrm from Cetyri (though this is secret)
3. Challenging the Estara directly before they complete their weapon

## The Trickster's Blessing

Yrk can provide protection from Milicent's gaze, making recipients safe from her tracking on the Material Plane. Without this protection, traveling on the Material Plane risks immediate detection and capture. This is accomplished by severing a creature's spiritual tie to the world where they were born. They can then be shielded from observation and -- importantly -- travel from world to world more easily.

## Contradictions in Her Methods

While Milicent fights to destroy the Estara (who enslave mortals), she uses the Shadow Projector to enslave mortals herself, creating the very conditions she once rebelled against. The Rebel Fey (and Milicent herself) regret her methods but have given her tacit permission to go on. The Estarans must be defeated.

## Relationship to the Party

Milicent has been looking for promising people to join her army against the Estara. She was interested in Gullux and was testing him, but the party defeated him. Now she is testing and observing the party through her shadow projections and Quickling servants, pulling them into shadow spaces to fight Gullux and Maxxa'Valien.

### Future Alliance

In a later chapter, the party meets Milicent directly and makes an alliance with her. As part of this alliance, they convince her to stop growing her army using the Shadow Projector.
