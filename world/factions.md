# Factions and Organizations

## The Counsel of Citizens

Governing body of the Allied Cities. Composed of Counselors from member cities who convene for conferences when needed. The first Citizen Conference in many years has been called, suggesting rising concerns.

## The Rebellion

Historical conflict in which mortals rose up against the Estara. Key figures included Maelric Sturmwald, the Ancient Dragon Gallifax, and Aldrin Stoneward. The Rebellion resulted in the burning of Metgal and the eventual freedom of the Allied Cities.

## Milicent's Forces

### The Archshade Milicent

A powerful figure raising an army in the Shadowfell to fight the Estarans before they can return and enact their doomsday plan. She holds an Estaran prisoner who revealed the plan. She seeks to recruit powerful individuals, including Erol and Lamat. Uses Quicklings as servants and agents.

### Planned Alliance (Future)

Milicent envisions an alliance of the Citizen Counsel, Naeliste, herself, and the Rebel Fey to stand against the Estarans.

## Fey of the Faedwood

Native inhabitants of the Faedwood including faeries, pixies, sprites, and other woodland creatures. Traditionally allied with Oechenfor but recently hostile due to Dragonborn hunting them for sport. Can be convinced to aid mortals if shown sympathy and respect.

## Dragonborn Clans

White Dragonborn from one or two related clans inhabiting the East Hills. Speak a crude dialect of Draconic. Came down to the Uplands for sport and profit under Gullux's leadership. Band of fifty warriors including scouts, bandits, druids, and wolf riders.

## Lome Monastery

Founded and patronized by Lamat. Led by the orc ascetic F'zuga. Consists of a dozen orc monks who train for town defense, craft tools for farmers, and lead ceremonies honoring service. The monastery includes a courtyard, three residential buildings, and a meeting hall.
