# Historical Timeline

## Ancient History (2000 Years Ago)

### The Estara Arrival

The Estara arrived on this world approximately 2000 years ago. They were powerful beings who ruled over mortals and gifted certain individuals with ageless, enduring life. Aldrin Stoneward was among those who received this gift before the First Rebellion.

### The First Rebellion (~2000 Years Ago)

Shortly after the Estarans arrived, the Fey and mortals began resisting. This was the First War, led by the seven Rebel Fey including Milicent. The conflict lasted for an extended period. Yrk the Trickster fought in this war, surviving through misdirection and sabotage.

### The Doomsday Plan

The Estarans developed a plan that would result in the destruction of all mortal life. Milicent holds an Estaran prisoner who revealed this plan. The Estara have annihilated the Material Plane 54 times to quell rebellions and defeat dragon singularities.

## The Rebellion (Ended 150 Years Ago)

A more recent conflict in which mortals rose up against continuing Estaran oppression. Key participants included:
- Maelric Sturmwald
- The Ancient Dragon Gallifax
- Aldrin Stoneward (veteran from ancient times)
- Erol the Faed
- Caera and Thorn (Aelira's parents)
- Lamat Velu
- Other veterans who became Founders

The Rebellion included the burning of Metgal, a catastrophic event with massive mortal casualties. The Rebellion ended 150 years ago, successfully freeing the Allied Cities from direct Estaran rule.

## Post-Rebellion Era (Last 150 Years)

### Era of Peace

Following the Rebellion's end 150 years ago, the Allied Cities enjoyed peace under the governance of the Counsel of Citizens and protection of Gallifax.

### Foundation of Oechenfor (~20 Years Ago)

Aldrin Stoneward, Erol the Faed, and other Rebellion veterans founded Oechenfor as a frontier city.

### Foundation of Lome (5 Years Ago)

Erol, Lamat Velu, Kruftus Unter, and others established Lome in the Uplands. Lamat founded the monastery and mentored F'zuga.

## Recent Events

### Last Autumn

Erol and Lamat disappeared after discovering they were targeted for recruitment by Milicent. They have been moving among refuges in the Feywild and Shadowfell since.

### Winter

Gullux stole white dragon eggs from Torma the Unforgiving's clutch. Fifty White Dragonborn warriors descended from the East Hills to the Uplands. They took the Upland Stair and began harassing Lome and hunting Fey in the Faedwood.

### Early Spring

The current events of Chapter 1 take place. No word from Lome since winter thaw. The first Citizen Conference in years is called. Aldrin Stoneward calls for volunteers to investigate before departing for Aris.
