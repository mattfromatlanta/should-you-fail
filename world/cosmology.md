# Planes and Cosmology

## The Material Plane

The primary world where the campaign takes place. Home to the Allied Cities, the Faedwood, the Uplands, and mortal civilizations.

## The Shadowfell

A dark reflection of the Material Plane. Milicent maintains a fortress here where she raises an army to fight the Estarans. Travel to the Shadowfell is described as perilous, with hostile environments, dangerous terrain, and threats that erode safety by existing there.

## The Feywild

A vibrant reflection of the Material Plane. Home to Fey creatures and characterized by intense magical energy. Erol and Lamat currently hide in refuges here. Naeliste is a location in the Feywild relevant to future story arcs. Xilo, Lord of Robins, is a trusted contact of Milicent who resides here.

## Foreign Worlds (Future)

The campaign will eventually involve travel to foreign worlds to assemble the Gold Dragon Singularity. The party will confront other versions of the dragon Cetyri across different settings. Some worlds are occupied by Estarans.

## Planar Travel

### Rings of Return

Magical items that show pathways between locations and planes. In Milicent's shadow reflection space, these rings show only fragmented, chaotic paths with no direct connection to the Natural World.

## Notable Beings

### The Estara/Estarans

Powerful beings who once ruled mortals and can traverse planes. They inflicted massive destruction on mortals at the end of the Rebellion. They possess a doomsday plan and may return to enact it. They currently occupy some foreign worlds.

### Dragons

Ancient and powerful beings allied with mortals. Gallifax protects the Allied Cities. Cetyri is a lost dragon key to Milicent's plan—using his instances across worlds to invoke the Gold Dragon Singularity, a weapon to eliminate the Estarans.

### The Rebel Fey

Leaders in the Feywild--Animal Lords and the like. They led the fight against the Estarans in both rebellions. Milicent was once one of them.

### Archshades

Powerful beings like Milicent who can manipulate reality and create reflection spaces. Milicent can shift beings into shadowy test environments and slay enemies without leaving physical evidence.
