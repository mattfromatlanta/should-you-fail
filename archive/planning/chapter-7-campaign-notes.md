## Second Half: Journey to Metgal

### Setup

**Departure:** Party leaves Aris with united world behind them

**Destination:** Metgal - location of Estaran ruin mentioned in Yrk's letter to Milicent

**Objective:** Find and enter the Radiant Projector, use it to launch cross-world journey

### Story Beats

- Travel to Metgal
- Locating the Estaran ruin
- Entering the heart of the ruin
- Activating/using the Radiant Projector
- First cross-world transit

## Long-Term Planning (Chapter 8+)

### First Off-World Quest

**World Characteristics:**
- Much lower population than home world
- Recently abandoned Estaran garrison
- Settlement under siege

**Antagonists:**
- Torma the Unforgiving (exists on this world too)
- Gullux (full dragon in this reality)
- Ice demons

**Complications:**
- Locals assume party are Estaran rescuers
- This world's Cetyri protecting the settlement
- Party must navigate misunderstanding

**Questions to Resolve:**
- How does this world's Cetyri fit in?
- Why did Estarans abandon garrison?
- What is Torma's role in this world?

## World State Developments

### Milicent and Yrk's Project

**Portable Radiant Projector:**
- Being designed for party's use
- Allows controlled world-hopping
- Alternative to fixed Estaran installations

**Star Model:**
- Navigation tool for cosmos
- Helps locate Estaran civilization
- Helps locate other Cetyris across realities

**Timeline:** A few weeks until ready

### Yrk and Felicity

**Current Activity:** Traveling mortal world

**Aris Arrival:** A few days after party arrives

**Purpose:** Deliver completed projector/star model, provide technical support

### Milicent

**Activity:** Making calculations and revisions in Shadow realm

**Status:** Honoring agreement - no mortal conscription

**Role:** Technical/magical support for the quest

### The Estarans

**Current State:** Facing increasing unrest across cosmos

**Resources:** Shifting to maintain peace

**Growing Sentiment:** This universe failing to meet harmonious potential

**Threat:** Reset increasingly considered as solution

**Timeline Pressure:** Party must act before Estarans decide to enact 56th apocalypse
