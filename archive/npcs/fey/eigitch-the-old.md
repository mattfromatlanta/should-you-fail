# Eigitch the Old

## Basic Information
**Race:** Goblin
**Age:** 173 years
**Role:** Storyteller, Adventurer, Submarine Captain
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 3
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

A lean, sharp-eyed goblin well into his "daring years." His cloak is frayed but decorated with dozens of small, mismatched objects—some charms, some tools, some likely jokes at someone's expense. The oldest goblin in Naeliste and its greatest storyteller.

## Background

Eigitch is the great-great-grandson of Yrk the Trickster (known as Ar'gix in the Drekklands). Only Eigitch and Yrk remember Yrk's true name. He fought in the First War against the Estara.

## Personality

Clever, darkly funny, and always ready for one more foolhardy adventure. He might spring a trap just to see how loud it snaps. Loves pranks and storytelling, often delivering information through misdirection.

## Knowledge

- Knows the Bullywugs have a secret path to the Drekklands (the Underwet)
- Suspects anyone who takes that path will be safe from Milicent while on it
- Knows much about the dangers of the Drekklands (black dragons, Svirfneblin hunters)
- Knows far less about the underground river passage itself, which is why he wants to go
- Tells stories of a powerful, unnamed figure (Yrk) from his youth

## Current Activities

Has constructed a submarine called "The Rainbow Trout" to navigate the Underwet. Itching for another adventure and will happily take the party to find the Trickster. He knows stories others have forgotten and may point the party toward the Rebel Fey indirectly.

## Pranks

Won't immediately tell the party about the submarine or that the path is underwater. Sends them to ask the Bullywugs, who know Eigitch is pranking them and send the party back.

## Reputation

- Goblins are taking bets on whether this journey will finally kill him
- Some believe Eigitch will live forever
- "If the Yeti didn't get him, nothing will"

## Current Status (End of Chapter 6)

Serving as messenger/contact point in Naeliste between Yrk and the party. Delivered Yrk's message about working on something important and meeting up in a few weeks. Relationship with party: Trusted contact and facilitator.
