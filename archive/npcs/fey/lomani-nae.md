# Lomani Nae, the Doorkeeper

## Basic Information
**Race:** Eladrin (Male)
**Role:** Cleric of Severance, Doorkeeper, Keeper of Rites
**Status:** Active
**First Appearance:** Chapter 3
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

Sentinel of sacred boundaries. Male Eladrin of solemn bearing. He is Naeliste's keeper of rites, overseeing ceremonies that mark the beginnings and endings of things: births, deaths, oaths, partings.

## Philosophy

Where many Eladrin revere the flow between seasons and realms, Lomani venerates the lines that divide. To him, the holiness of life lies in distinction—each soul, realm, and rite holding its own shape and silence. His clerical power is drawn from the spirits of Severance, the unseen forces that guard the borders of existence.

## Role

Created and maintains Naeliste's permanent teleportation circle. He is responsible for the city's magical travel infrastructure and ensures proper boundaries are maintained between realms and souls.

## Studies and Discoveries

### Eladrin Memory Research
Lomani has long studied the collective memory of Eladrin, which is connected by spiritual strands. This gives him unique insight into soul patterns.

### Cetyri's Examination
Through spiritual and magical observation, Lomani makes shocking discoveries about Cetyri:
1. Cetyri's eyesight is slowly returning
2. It's remarkable that he is undead but not wholly lost
3. Cetyri's spirit exhibits an irregular and dispersed pattern
4. His soul has strands reaching in many directions
5. Cetyri's spirit exists in multiple places/worlds simultaneously

Lomani wonders what it would mean to bring two of Cetyri's instances together.

## Personality

Solemn and careful. Approaches research methodically. Shows concern when making discoveries that could have cosmic implications.

## Impact on Story

His discovery of Cetyri's multiversal soul connections is crucial to understanding the dragon's potential as a Unary Great Wyrm. However, Lomani doesn't fully understand what this means and hasn't yet discussed it with Cetyri when he first makes the discovery.

## Note on Cetyri

When the party discusses Lomani's findings, Cetyri confirms he was aware of his condition. He was worried this is what Erol meant by "his choice." He warns that connecting his fragmented soul across realities could have disastrous effects—but may grant him the strength to destroy Milicent.
