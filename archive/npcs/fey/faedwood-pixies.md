# Faedwood Pixies

A group of pixies living in the Faedwood who were attacked by the Dragonborn invaders.

## Thorna

Sharp-tongued, protective, and highly suspicious of mortals. Quick to scold, but slower to trust. Acts like the de facto leader of the sprite band.

## Whindwhip

Restless and wild-hearted, with a love of movement and noise. Darts through trees like wind in leaves. Laughs often, especially at danger.

## Lintee

Gentle and dreamy, with a soft voice and an eye for beauty in unexpected places. Sings to flowers and follows butterflies mid-conversation.

## Snell

Quiet, thoughtful, and precise. Speaks rarely but always knows what others have forgotten. Seems tuned to distant or subtle magics.

## Elmleaf

**Status:** Rescued from the Nest

Brave and disciplined pixie warrior, wounded and exhausted when the party found him at the Nest in Chapter 1. He recovered swiftly. He and the other fey traveled with the party to the Upland Stair, keeping watch over the path behind them.
