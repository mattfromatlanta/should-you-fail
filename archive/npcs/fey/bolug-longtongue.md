# Bo'Lug Longtongue, the Swampknower

## Basic Information
**Race:** Bullywug
**Role:** Swampknower
**Status:** Active
**Location:** Naeliste, Foot of the Falls

## Description

A weathered old Bullywug who speaks in riddles and knows the swamps like a second skin. He watches everything in silence before he decides if you're worth speaking to.

## The Riddle

When the party visited Bo'Lug seeking information about the path to the Drekklands, he interrupted his own explanation to share a befuddling riddle or recommendation:

"Swamp sings different when the old roots stir. Mmm. Heard it last when the moon hid behind herself and the water forgot how to ripple. You want answers? Hah. Don't look for lanterns on the path—follow the oldest frog songs back home. That's where the forgotten lords listen, still wet with sorrow. They don't come when called. But they'll know when you're coming."

Melbara later recognized this riddle as connected to the Rebel Fey.

## The Underwet Path

It turns out that Eigitch knows all about the path to the Drekklands—Bo'Lug was somewhat bewildered that Eigitch would send the party to him. The path goes underwater through an underground river system that connects the Foot of the Falls to other places around the world. Eigitch has a submarine to make the journey.
