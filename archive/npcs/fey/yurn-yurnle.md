# Yurn Yurnle, Goblin Toymaker

## Basic Information
**Race:** Goblin
**Role:** Toymaker, Artificer
**Status:** Active
**Location:** Naeliste

## Background

Yurn was an illegally indentured servant of Gord Lankmangled in Oechenfor, forced to use his Artificer skills to make weapons. He was freed by Cormin Van Morgaaran and Taram Thristle when they exposed Gord's illegal operation.

Cormin tried to scrape together funds for Yurn to start a new business in Oechenfor that would employ him and his Artificer skills properly. However, money was tight, and Yurn decided to return home to Naeliste to pursue his true love of toymaking. Cormin gave him enough support to send him on his way. They haven't caught up since.
