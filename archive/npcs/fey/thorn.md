# Thorn

## Basic Information
**Race:** Eladrin
**Role:** Veteran of the Rebellion
**Status:** Unknown (disappeared)
**Relationship:** Caera's partner, Aelira's father

## Background

Thorn fought in the Rebellion alongside his partner Caera. Together they worked to create a world their daughter Aelira could walk through without fear. He and Caera were in the field together when the Rebellion was ending.

## Disappearance

From Caera's letter written near the end of the Rebellion: "Thorn thinks something's changed" regarding the Estara giving ground. Shortly after this letter was written, Thorn disappeared. His current status and whereabouts are unknown.

## Personality

From Caera's letter: During the Rebellion, Thorn found a wooden sword meant for a child in a burned-out halfling village and "didn't say anything for a long time." He missed Aelira deeply during the war.

## Note

Thorn is mentioned only in Caera's letter to Melbara from the Rebellion. His fate after the war and current status remain a mystery. Aelira may not have many memories of her father if he disappeared when she was very young.
