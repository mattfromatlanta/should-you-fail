# Javva Nae, the Quiet Branch

## Basic Information
**Race:** Eladrin
**Role:** Ancestral witness and steward of House Nae
**Status:** Active
**Location:** Naeliste

## Description

Javva is the Quiet Branch of House Nae, the ancient Eladrin family that founded Naeliste nearly two millennia ago. Though she holds no formal power, her presence is like the axle of a turning wheel—the silent point around which the city's many lives revolve. Javva is the ancestral witness and steward of boundless wild growth.
