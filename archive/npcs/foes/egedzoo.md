# Egedzoo

## Basic Information
**Race:** Dragon (Adult Blue)
**Role:** Would-be Wasteland Warlord
**Status:** Deceased (slain Chapter 6b) — *may appear on another world*
**First Appearance:** Chapter 6a
**Location:** Canyon lair in the Feywild Wasteland

## Description

An adult blue dragon who sought to carve out a kingdom in the Feywild's scorched wasteland by uniting the hobgoblin bandit factions under his rule. Cunning, territorial, and contemptuous of Xilo's influence. He fought on his home ground with every advantage he had prepared—and was still outmatched.

## Background

Egedzoo had identified the devastated outer Feywild as an opportunity: a power vacuum left by Estaran destruction, full of organized hobgoblin armies with no permanent lord. He began consolidating them through a combination of fear and strategic patience. Xilo dispatched Twi'Xu to monitor him before deciding whether to act.

The party first encountered Egedzoo at Rinick's Diner, where he and Twi'Xu had reached a silent standoff. Respecting the neutral-ground truce—and unwilling to provoke an Animal Lord without greater advantage—Egedzoo withdrew. Later, he attacked the party's airship with hobgoblin sky-riders mid-flight, damaging the vessel and forcing a crash near Ozevriel's memorial. He was driven off but not slain.

After the party's assault on his canyon lair, where Cormin's illusion tricked him into wasting his breath weapon on a decoy, Aelira pinned him in summoned webbing and the party finished him quickly.

## The Canyon Lair

Egedzoo's lair was a deep canyon carved into the wasteland, approximately 200 feet deep and 100 feet wide at the top. He had prepared it with:

- An artificial electrical storm over the canyon, with lightning strike hazards
- Electrified water pools on the canyon floor
- A defended entrance plateau with hobgoblin devastators and iron shadows
- A narrow trapped passage leading inward
- A guard chamber with a hobgoblin warlord and soldiers
- A vast inner sanctum with an elevated perch, unstable floor sections, and a lightning rod he could activate as a bonus action

## Known Intelligence

Egedzoo had discovered Estaran ruins deeper in the wasteland while establishing his territory. A dormant Radiant Projector lies in those ruins, partially buried. Whether this information was shared with the party before his death is a matter of session record.

## Combat Notes

- **CR:** 16 (Adult Blue Dragon)
- **HP:** 225 | **AC:** 19
- **Lightning Breath** (Recharge 5-6): 12d10 lightning damage, 90-foot line, DC 19 Dex save
- **Frightful Presence:** DC 19 Wisdom save
- **Legendary Actions:** 3 per round
- His lair conducted lightning through a web of embedded rods—a hazard Cormin's illusion revealed before the party entered

## Fate

Slain in his sanctum during the Chapter 6b assault. *Per DM notes, he may be encountered again on another world.*
