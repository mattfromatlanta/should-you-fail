# Erol and Lamat

## Basic Information
**Race:** Erol - Eladrin Fey; Lamat - Unknown
**Role:** Veterans of the Rebellion, Founders of Oechenfor
**Status:** Missing
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Encounter 06 (mentioned)

## Description

Erol is described as "Erol the Faed" and is renowned as perhaps the greatest among the Founders of Oechenfor. The pair live together in an elegant Eladrin-style manor atop the Teeth.

## Background

Both are veterans of the Rebellion and Founders of Oechenfor who chose to live in solitude with a majestic view of the Faedwood. Lamat founded and patronized the monastery in Lome, mentoring F'zuga five years ago.

## Status

Missing since autumn. They fled suddenly after discovering they were targeted for recruitment (abduction) by the Archshade Milicent. Rather than be captured, they are moving among refuges in the Feywild and Shadowfell. Their manor was left to give the impression they had just stepped out.

## Return and Heroic Action

Erol and Lamat return at the end of Chapter 2. They kill Torma the Unforgiving, saving the party and Lome from the dragon's wrath.

## Belongings

Their manor contains extradimensional storage, enchanted items, and a collection of spirits from across the multiverse. Lamat left behind his Wraps of Unarmed Power +1 and 100pp plus 5 rubies (200gp each) in traveling money. Erol's Tome of Epicurean Delights and an Immovable Rod remain in the garden shed.
