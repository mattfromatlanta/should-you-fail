# Chapter 1: Invaders

## Overview

The Allied Cities have been at peace for a generation, governed by the Counsel of Citizens and protected by alliance with the ancient dragon Gallifax. In the frontier city of Oechenfor, Chief Citizen Aldrin Stoneward prepares to depart for the first Citizen Conference in years. Before leaving, he must address a local crisis: the village of Lome in the Uplands has gone silent, and the Faedwood has turned hostile to travelers.

Aldrin calls for volunteers to investigate. The party answers and discovers a band of White Dragonborn invaders led by the monstrous Gullux has taken the Upland Stair and laid siege to Lome. During their final confrontation with Gullux, the party is pulled into a shadowy test created by the Archshade Milicent. Upon victory, they awaken to find all remaining invaders mysteriously slain.

## Story Threads

- First encounter with agents of Milicent
- Discovery of dragon eggs stolen from Torma the Unforgiving
- Disappearance of Oechenfor Founders Erol and Lamat
- Tension between Fey of the Faedwood and mortal settlements

## Chapter Outcomes

The party defeats Gullux and breaks the siege of Lome by fighting him in a shadow projection of his hideout. They encounter Milicent's Shadow Projector for the first time, where they fought Gullux and heard her Quicklings laughing at them from the walls. However, they left the Shadow Projection without gaining any insight into what it was or the nature of their capture.

Back in Gullux's hideout they found three white dragon eggs taken from Torma the Unforgiving, Adult White Dragon and mother of Gullux—a half-dragon and a dragon grafter. The party burned the three eggs. The Founders Erol and Lamat remain missing.
