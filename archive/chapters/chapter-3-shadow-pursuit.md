# Chapter 3: The Shadow Pursuit

## Overview

After Erol and Lamat slay Torma the Unforgiving, the party escapes the Underdark with their new allies Bronik and Cetyri. Erol reveals that Milicent was her first patron and that Milicent is reactivating the ritual that created the Dread Legion centuries ago. She directs the party to seek the Trickster's Blessing in Naeliste, which will protect them from Milicent's gaze.

In Naeliste, the party has time to research, rest, and plan. They can pursue multiple paths: seek the Trickster's Blessing through Eigitch's submarine journey, contact the Rebel Fey, challenge Milicent directly at the Shadow Projector, help Cetyri understand his potential as a Unary Great Wyrm, or return to investigate the crisis in Oechenfor/Aris.

The chapter features extensive exploration of Naeliste's culture, key revelations from ancient texts, and the perilous journey through the Underwet to the Drekklands to meet Yrk the Trickster.

## Key Revelations

### Milicent's Past and Present
- Milicent was one of the seven original Rebel Fey who fought the Estara
- She was Erol's first patron before their bond broke
- She is resurrecting the ritual/device that created the Dread Legion
- She built the Shadow Projector with Yrk ~400 years ago
- She is fighting a secret war against the Estara
- The Estara are preparing a massive weapon to control their vast empire

### The Shadow Projector
- A corrupted adaptation of ancient Estaran Radiant Projectors
- Uses the Material Plane as a lens (the "Essential Knot")
- Projects concentrated darkness across realities
- Captures creatures who pass through its projection
- Gradually transforms captives into shadow beings
- Located in Milicent's mountain fortress in the Shadowfell

### Cetyri's Potential
- Dragon souls reach across realities to other versions of themselves
- Cetyri's damaged soul makes him uniquely able to safely unify with his other selves
- A dragon who binds all versions becomes a Unary Great Wyrm
- Unary Great Wyrms are among the most powerful cosmic forces
- They can be truly killed (unlike regular dragons)
- The Estara fear them (Varkhasia the red great worm nearly destroyed them)
- This has been Milicent's intent for Cetyri all along

### The Rebel Fey
- Seven original Archfey and Beast Lords: Itrina, Carris, Xilo, Ozevriel, Thessa, Varuun, Milicent
- Ozevriel sacrificed himself to let the other six escape
- Five remain (without Ozevriel and Milicent)
- They want Milicent stopped but relationship is complex
- They possess knowledge of the Shadow Projector
- They understand Cetyri's potential

### The First War
- The Fey fought the Estara from the moment they arrived
- Much of this history is lost to time
- The Fey fell back to the Faedwood to bottleneck the Estara
- Yrk the Trickster survived by avoiding direct confrontation

### The Estara's Power
- They once annihilated the entire Material Plane to end a rebellion
- They have conquered hundreds of worlds across a vast empire
- They keep worlds separate to prevent detection of their scale
- Veterans' fragmented memories result from this isolation strategy
- They are preparing another cosmic-scale weapon

## Story Paths

The party can pursue multiple directions:

### 1. Eigitch and the Trickster
Travel through the Underwet (underground river system) via submarine to the Drekklands to meet Yrk and receive the Trickster's Blessing, which protects from Milicent's gaze.

### 2. Find the Rebel Fey
Enter the deep Feywild and seek the Rebel Fey's aid against Milicent. They possess crucial knowledge but are not safe allies.

### 3. Break the Shadow Projector
Challenge Milicent directly by assaulting her fortress and destroying the Shadow Projector.

### 4. Cetyri's Choices
Help Cetyri understand his potential and choose between revenge against Milicent or becoming a Unary Great Wyrm.

### 5. Meanwhile in Aris
Return to investigate the crisis in Oechenfor (disappearances) or Aris (Maelric trapped in dream, doppelganger infiltration).

## Major Locations

### Naeliste
Fey city built beneath the Teeth cliffs where the West Falls thunder down. Home to Eladrin, Goblins, and Bullywugs. Features four seasonal towers, Goblinville carved into cliffs, Bullywug homes in the marshland, and the Library of Rhymes and Antiquities.

### The Underwet
Eigitch's name for the underground river system flowing beneath the Allied Cities. Secret path to the Drekklands.

### Bullhome
Ruined keep on a lake in the Drekklands, temporarily occupied by Bullywug refugees from Gulpfen Brood.

### The Drekklands
Vast, uncomfortable swamp full of black dragon nests, Svirfneblin tribes, dinosaurs, and magical instability. Home of Yrk the Trickster.

## Important Documents

### The Testament of Embers
Ancient Estaran epic describing Varkhasia the red Unary Great Wyrm who nearly destroyed the Estara. His defeat came through the Estara's "Last Resort" - annihilation of the Material Plane.

### Letter from Grib to Yip
Personal letter from the First War showing desperation of fighting the Estara and the strategic withdrawal to the Faedwood.

### The Ode to Seven Lords
Formal poem detailing the seven original Rebel Fey and Milicent's fall from grace.

### Letter from Caera to Melbara
Written during the Rebellion, expressing longing for Aelira and hope for victory.

## Key Discoveries

### Lomani's Discovery
Through spiritual examination, Lomani discovers that Cetyri's spirit exhibits an irregular, dispersed pattern with strands reaching to many worlds. His spirit exists in multiple realities simultaneously.

### Melbara's Collection
Contains the Testament of Embers, showing precedent for a Unary Great Wyrm threatening the Estara.

### Bo'Lug's Riddle
A conditional invitation to seek the Rebel Fey, possibly sent by them directly.

## Warnings and Dangers

- Without the Trickster's Blessing, the party risks capture by Milicent's servants
- Erol warns: Milicent cannot track the party in Naeliste, but anywhere else on the Material Plane they can be seen
- Oechenfor has infiltration by Quicklings abducting townsfolk
- Aris is overrun with doppelgangers loyal to Milicent
- Maelric is trapped in a permanent dream
- The Drekklands are filled with black dragons, hostile tribes, and magical dangers

## Chapter Status

This chapter presents multiple branching paths with no single required sequence. The party can pursue goals in various orders based on their priorities and discoveries.

## Outcome

The party is pursued by Milicent's forces but do make their way to Naeliste. The party discover the documents in the library and learn more history. Critically, they find the Ode to Seven Lords and the Testament of Embers. These contain key lore details that will guide them later in the campaign. They connect with Eigitch the Old who, despite a few practical jokes, is eager to take them to meet the Trickster. Cytiri is examined by Lomani Nae who discovers his soul seems to reach out across space... Bronik finds friends with the Goblins and decides to stay. The party plan to travel to the Drekklands with Eigitch ... through the underground rivers ... by submarine!
