# Underdark Locations

## Access Points

### East Hills Cave System

The caves in the East Hills where Gullux's band hid contain passages leading deep underground to the Underdark. After Gullux's defeat, Kruftus and F'zuga explored and found several caves, some going deep underground. These caves served as refuge when Lome evacuated to hide from Torma.

## Kobold Ruins

Ancient hiding place of kobolds who once served Milicent but fled underground. They were hunted and killed by her Death Knight champions. The ruins contain:

### Room Features
1. **Sleeping room** - Dirty hides, broken door, evidence of fire trap, secret door behind brazier leading to Hidden Storage
2. **Storage area** - Moldy boxes, empty cistern, secret door with acid spray trap
3. **Workshop/Shrine** - Mimics disguised as ore crate, brazier, backpack, and two-headed dragon shrine. Curses to "Shadowdame M" written in Draconic on walls
4. **Hidden Storage** - Gold and silver ore
5. **Precious Items** - Silver and gold bars, Heward's Handy Haversack

### Inhabitants
- Piercers clinging to ceilings
- Ropers posing as stalagmites
- Mimics disguised as objects
- Hook Horrors
- Zombies
- Green Slime
- Disorienting mushrooms (poison effect)
- Luminous mushrooms growing from kobold skeletons

## The Beholders' Realm

A great cave system in the Underdark with an underground canyon split evenly between rival beholders.

### Geography
- Long underground canyon with waterfall
- Cold, clean lake in the middle
- Mushroom forests where Myconid workers pile organic matter
- Neutral zone in the center (territory dispute)

### Fortresses
- **Maxxa'Valien's Keep** - Fortress at one end of the canyon
- **Maxxa'Volien's Keep** - Identical fortress at opposite end

Both fortresses are perfectly matched as part of their territorial treaty.

### Inhabitants
- Maxxa'Valien (beholder)
- Maxxa'Volien (beholder)
- Young deep dragons (pair in disputed territory)
- Myconids
- Various fungi (violet fungus, necrohulk, shrieker fungus, gas spore fungus)
- Oozes (black pudding, ochre jelly)
- Zombies
- Undead sludge

### The Lake

Cold, clean water where creatures can swim. Appears to be where Cetyri's discarded body was carried after his death in Milicent's dungeon. The deep dragons have established their lair overlapping the neutral zone by "at least a dozen feet."

## Notable Features

- Water flows from surface through falls into the lake
- Organic decay supports mushroom ecosystem
- Territory precisely divided by ancient beholder treaty
- Connection to surface through multiple cave passages
