# Chapter 2: Darkness

## Overview

Following their victory over Gullux, the party helps Lome begin recovery. Their respite is short-lived when Bronik, the half-dragon brother of Gullux, arrives seeking the stolen treasure and dragon eggs. He warns that Torma the Unforgiving is coming for her stolen offspring. To survive the dragon's wrath, Lome evacuates to the caves in the East Hills.

The caves lead deep into the Underdark, where the party accidentally invades the realm of Maxxa'Valien, a beholder locked in a territorial dispute with his twin Maxxa'Volien. The party is enlisted to clear deep dragons from neutral territory. In the depths, they encounter Cetyri, a blind gold dragon revenant who was Milicent's prisoner for a century.

When they return to the beholder's lair, Milicent pulls them back into her shadow space to face a transformed Maxxa'Valien. The chapter ends with Torma collapsing the cave entrance and Milicent's agents offering escape: "Come with us if you want to live."

## Locations

### East Hills Cave System
The caves where Gullux's band hid now serve as refuge for Lome's citizens. Passages lead deep underground to the Underdark.

### Kobold Ruins
Ancient hiding place of kobolds who fled from Milicent's service. They were hunted by her Death Knight champions. Contains trapped rooms, secret doors, mimics, and curses written to "Shadowdame M."

### The Underdark Realm
A great cave system with an underground canyon, waterfall, and lake. Home to the rival beholders and various fungi, oozes, and undead creatures.

### Maxxa'Valien's Keep
Fortress at one end of the underground canyon. Identical to Maxxa'Volien's fortress at the opposite end.

### The Lake
Cold, clean water in the middle of the canyon. Contains the where Cetyri's body was carried after death.

## Key Revelations

- Gullux was Torma's son who betrayed her by stealing eggs
- Bronik identifies stolen treasure: adamantine armor and flaming sword once belonged to Garrik the Bold who challenged Torma
- Erol is female and has not been seen in Naeliste (Feywild location)
- Kobolds once served Milicent but fled; she sent Death Knights after them
- Cetyri was Milicent's prisoner for a century, used to train Death Knights
- Cetyri is blind and doesn't know where he is.
- The beholders were born when an unknown god's nightmare split in two

## Chapter Outcomes

The party captures Bronik while he searches for his dead brother Gullux. From Bronik, they discover that Torma the Unforgiving will be arriving in the Uplands soon with vengeance on her mind. They make a plan with the leaders of Lome for the party to look for shelter for the townsfolk in the East Hills.

In their exploration, they find a path into the Underdark and explore. They find a kobold ruin and see discover a reference to a nemesis Shadowdame M. This is Milicent, though it is not yet clear to the party.

They make their way deeper and enter the realm of Maxxa'Valien and Maxxa'Volien. They encounter Maxxa'Valien and make a deal to rid him of an invading creature that has settled between their keeps. They make their way to the lake in the middle of their realm and discover Cytiri, the Gold Dragon revanent who was Milicent's prisoner.

When they return to Maxxa'Valien's keep, they are again dragged into a Shadow Projection where they must face the beholder in combat -- though he has been weakend some by Milicent to level the field.

After defeating the transformed Maxxa'Valien in Milicent's shadow space, they discover Cetyri, a blind gold dragon revenant. Torma attacks and is killed by Erol and Lamat at the chapter's end. Bronik begins to consider that his capture might have set him free... Erol and Lamat lead the people of Lome south to Oechenfor. The party follows down the Upland Stair with Cytiri and Bronik, but they turn east on Erol's advice. They seek shelter from Milicent's eye in Naeliste, and a path to seek the Trickster's Blessing.
