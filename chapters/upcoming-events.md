# Upcoming Events

This document tracks planned future events, potential storylines, and narrative threads to explore in upcoming sessions and chapters.

## Immediate: Chapter 6b Conclusion

**Status**: Ready to run

The party must assault Egedzoo's lair in the Feywild wasteland. Xilo will draw away the hobgoblin army while the party infiltrates the dragon's canyon stronghold and faces him in combat.

**Outcomes**:
- Defeat Egedzoo (or drive him off)
- Secure Xilo's alliance
- Claim the dragon's hoard
- Return to Material Plane with Cetyri and Bronik
- Gain Feywild Compass from Xilo

## Short Term: Return to the Material Plane

**Status**: Needs development

After returning from the Feywild, the party will need to:
- Decide on next steps in building the coalition against the Estarans
- Follow up on leads from Milicent's research
- Continue preparing Cetyri for his role as the Dragon Singularity

**Potential Immediate Hooks**:
- Investigation of Metgal and the northern regions (where Estarans first arrived)

## Medium Term: The Search for Radiant Projectors

**Narrative Potential**:
- Journey to Metgal or the northern regions
- Locate dormant or active Estaran technology
- Study how Radiant Projectors work
- Potentially capture or repurpose one
- Discover more about Estaran capabilities and vulnerabilities

**Notes**:
- Egedzoo mentioned encountering Estaran ruins deeper in the Feywild wasteland with a dormant Radiant Projector (if party negotiated with him)

## Medium Term: Building the Coalition

**Status**: Ongoing thread

The party must unite disparate factions against the Estaran threat:

**Allies Secured**:
- Milicent and the Shadowfell forces (conditional alliance)
- Xilo and some Rebel Fey
- Cetyri (committed but needs time)

**Potential Allies to Pursue**:
- Other Rebel Fey (five remain after Ozevriel's death and Milicent's departure)
- Dragon councils or individual powerful dragons
- Leadership of the Allied Cities
- Other Fey courts and powers
- Ancient beings who remember the first rebellion

**Challenges**:
- Many factions distrust Milicent
- The Estaran threat seems distant after 52 years of dormancy
- Convincing others of the escalating pattern and urgency

## Medium Term: Cetyri's Development

**Status**: Character arc in progress

Cetyri has committed to the cause but needs:
- Time to process his role and responsibilities
- Training or guidance on how to merge his multiverse-spanning souls
- Understanding of what becoming a Unary Great Wyrm actually means
- Emotional support and friendship as he faces his destiny

**Narrative Potential**:
- Quests to help Cetyri grow in power and confidence
- Encounters with other dragons who can guide or challenge him
- Discovering more about dragon multiplicity and the Singularity process
- Bronik's role as friend and supporter

## Long Term: Preparing for the Estaran Return

**Status**: Campaign endgame building

According to Milicent's research, the Estarans will return. The party must:
- Ensure Cetyri can become the Dragon Singularity
- Assemble the coalition of allies
- Develop weapons and strategies using necrotic energy
- Locate and study Estaran technology
- Strengthen the Material Plane's defenses
- Prepare for reality-level conflict

**Key Questions**:
- When exactly will the Estarans return?
- Can the party take proactive steps, or must they wait for the invasion?
- What triggers the transformation into a Dragon Singularity?
- How many allies are needed to survive the coming war?

## Story Threads to Follow Up

### The Five Remaining Rebel Fey
- Xilo is one; five others remain after Ozevriel's death and Milicent's departure
- Each could be a powerful ally or source of ancient knowledge
- Potential for individual story arcs seeking each one out

### The Weakened Feywild
- Xilo explained that Ozevriel's death and Milicent's departure weakened the realm
- The scorched wastelands and thorn vines persist because the Feywild lacks strength to heal
- Could the party help restore the Feywild's strength?
- What would that require?

### Milicent's Continued Research
- She's been in the Shadowfell for centuries studying the Estarans
- What else has she learned?
- Can the party maintain communication with her?
- Is Yrk still in contact with her?

### The Mystery of the 54 Apocalypses
- How much do others know about this?
- Are there survivors or records from previous iterations?
- Can the party find more evidence to convince skeptics?

### Gallifax and the Dragons
- The gold dragon Gallifax is mentioned in campaign context as an "ancient and powerful dragon ally"
- When and how does the party connect with Gallifax?
- What role do dragons play in the coming conflict?

### The Allied Cities and Mortal Leadership
- Does the Counsel of Citizens know about the Estaran threat?
- Should the party reveal what they've learned?
- How do mortal nations prepare for reality-level war?

### Yrk the Trickster's Role
- He's agreed to contact Milicent and facilitate communication
- He's nearly 2000 years old and remembers the first rebellion
- What else does he know that he hasn't shared?
- Could he become a more active ally?
