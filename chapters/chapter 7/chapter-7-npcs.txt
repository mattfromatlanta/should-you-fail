# Chapter 7 NPCs

## Key Figures in Aris

**Aldrin Stoneward** - Chief Citizen of Oechenfor, attending the emergency Citizen Conference. Veteran of the Rebellion, trusted diplomat trying to hold fractious political factions together. Growing exhausted after two months of negotiations.

**Erol the Faed** - Founder of Oechenfor, warlock, Veteran of the Rebellion. Aldrin's closest political ally in Aris. Has been helping manage relations with the Fey-aligned delegates.

Erol was once Milicent's warlock and now serves Xilo, who is coming with the party to Aris.

**Lamat** - Erol's companion, monk. Quiet, formidable presence. Lamat 

**Chief Exarch Maelric Sturmwald** - Human wizard of immense power and renown, appears in his 50s but nearly 200 years old. Veteran of Estaran wars on other worlds (though he has no memory of this service). Closest friend and ally to Gallifax. Most prominent political leader since before the Rebellion. Currently trapped in a comatose trance in the Hall of Allies.

**Gallifax** - Ancient bronze dragon, Maelric's closest friend and ally. Has claimed the Hall of Allies as his lair and refuses to let anyone near the Chief Exarch. Ostensibly neutral in political conflicts but immovably loyal to Maelric.

**Nava Kurn** - Dwarf woman, 45 years old. Charismatic leader of the Young Bastards political faction. Sharp-tongued, pragmatic, frustrated with Veteran dominance. Sees the current crisis as proof the old guard must step aside.

**Ardo Manessi** - Human city councilor, 47 years old. Ambitious political operator maneuvering to become Maelric's successor. Publicly supports the Veterans while privately courting Young Bastard allies. **Secret:** Looking for the opportunity to kill or have killed Maelric. Hopes to maneuver chaos into personal power.

## Party Allies

**Yrk the Trickster** - Ancient goblin, will arrive unexpectedly a few days after the party with Felicity and their machine.

**Felicity** - Human Warlock. Took Erol's place as Milicent's servant. Traveling with Yrk as Milicent's voice.

**Cetyri** - Young gold dragon whose soul has been successfully untethered. Milicent hopes he can become the Singularity to destroy the Estarans. 

**Bronik** - Half white dragon, traveling with the party. Son of Torma the Unforgiving.

**Xilo** - Archfey, Lord of Beasts, traveling with the party as envoy of the Rebel Fey. He will be the first Fey lord to travel to the Mortal capital.
