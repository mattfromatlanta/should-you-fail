# Chapter 7: Encounters

## 1. Arrival

The party arrives in Aris with their funny entourage. Twix'u has gone ahead to make arrangements. They plan to meet him but he is late. While they wait, they witness an ugly argument between two veterans and a crowd of laborers. 

# Depending on player priority:

## Aldrin + Ardo Manessi

Aldrin has been staying in a city guest house in the High Ward. He's been working to keep peace, putting off any decisions about a change in leadership as long as he can. He can let the party know about Maelric's condition and give them some insight into where things stand around town

- Gallifax's private vigil at the Hall of Allies
- The Young Bastards' unrest, and what he knows about their leader, Nava Kurn.
- His own thoughts on the Veterans' ongoing political dominance

Aldrin is somewhat caught. His own feeling is that driving the Estarans away was a victory won too hard to relinquish. He thinks an active, ongoing defensive posture is not only reasonable but necessary. But he's running out of things to say to people who have gone their entire lives without even seeing an Estaran. The only people who remember them now are Veterans and Fey.

Aldrin is taking regular council from Ardo Manessi. He has no idea that Ardo has been stirring up trouble around town, hoping to take power for himself

## Erol + Xilo

Erol is also staying in the High Ward in similar arrangements to Aldrin. Lamat is with her.

Erol is trying to support Aldrin but she's skeptical that he can keep things calm much longer. She's sympathetic to the Young Bastards and their sympathizers. She didn't want to be permanently ruled by rigid soldiers and rulemakers either. She's hoping he'll give up and let Aris sort itself out but she hasn't succeeded yet.

Before they arrived, Xilo arranged to rent a manor close to Erol's accommodations. While Xilo is in town, Erol is bound to follow his orders. She spends much time initially making arrangments for him to meet prominent local people. Xilo hopes to leave Aris with a useful network here for the future. The party, Twix'u, Cytiri, and Bronik will stay there. On the way to Aris, Xilo has hired Bronik formally to his personal staff as a bodyguard.

## Maelric + Gallifax 

Maelric and Gallifax are at the Hall of Allies. Maelric is in a comatose trance and Gallifax stands guard. The dragon was the first to suspect that someone would try to take advantage to seize power and nothing that has happened since has convinced him otherwise. He is determined to enforce peace as long as possible and protect Maelric above all else. He has been physically preventing the Aris + Allied City counselors from formally convening.

## Nava Kurn

Nava is the leader of the Young Bastards political movement and the party can seek her out if they wish. Her view is simple. The Estarans are gone. Three generations of Mortals have come since and many who were born after the Estarans were defeated have died of old age. It's time to move on. No more deference to veterans. No more insistence on a permanent war posture. No more taxes supporting a standing army of broken pseudomortals.

She doesn't intend for matters to turn violent but she knows there are those on her side who want a fight...

## Yrk and Felicity

These two will arrive in Aris hours before the riot. They've made a breakthrough and they have a new device the party can use to cross worlds. They will insist it's time for the party to go.
