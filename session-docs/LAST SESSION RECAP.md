LAST SESSION RECAP — The Riot and the Dragon
============================================

The hundred Estaran warriors who descended on the waterfront at sunset were an illusion.
Aelira saw through it immediately. The Veterans were not so fortunate — many were struck
by a wave of psychic anguish at the sight of the radiant figures, and Erol was hit hardest
of all. You watched Aldrin charge his mount between her and the nearest Estaran, screaming,
before the whole apparition flickered and vanished.

The silence that followed lasted about two seconds. Then the drums started.

Somewhere in the city, Nava Kurn's demonstration had been waiting. Young Bastard chants
rolled down every street toward the waterfront. The Veterans — humiliated, frightened,
furious — poured back into the city looking for a fight. The riot had begun.

WHAT YOU DID
------------
You worked to slow the bleeding. Aelira and Cormin used spells to cool some Veterans down
before they could do real damage. Beacon made a compelling enough case that a few turned
around to rally their own for calm rather than violence. It wasn't enough to stop it — but
you may have kept the edges from fraying as fast as they could have.

Aldrin, when you reached him, was entirely occupied with Erol. He told you finding Nava
was probably the right move, but he couldn't tell you where she was, and he wasn't leaving
Erol's side.

FINDING NAVA
------------
Cormin used mass suggestion on a group of Young Bastards and let them lead you to her.
A trio of Veterans blocked your path and things nearly turned ugly — but you stared them
down without anyone getting hurt.

Nava was in a safehouse, pacing, barely holding herself together. She knew she had caused
a disaster. With her was a man named Ekert Shae — quiet, watchful, not especially
forthcoming. You convinced Nava to come with you to find Aldrin and Erol, making the case
that the two groups together could do more to calm the city than either could alone.
She agreed.

WHERE THINGS STAND
------------------
The city is on fire. Veterans and Young Bastards are at each other's throats. Aldrin and
Erol will be no more help tonight. You stand in the street with Nava Kurn and Ekert Shae,
still hoping you can do something to keep this from getting worse. Gallifax has left the
Hall of Citizens and circles the city. It's up to you what comes next...
