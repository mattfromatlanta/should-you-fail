E03: Shock of Cold
Status: COMPLETE

What happened:
- Party deployed the magic fortress immediately on arrival to shelter from the
  blizzard
- Investigated the "something missing" sensation — confirmed fire works, magic
  works; the absence remained unresolved and unexplained (mystery still open)
- Druidic magic used to read the storm; learned it would pass soon, and it did
- When the blizzard broke, Cetyri shifted to pseudodragon form without ceremony
  — compact and fast, copper-gold, golden eyes. Told the party this was the
  safest shape. He cannot predict how his ice world counterpart will react to
  encountering him; intends to stay small until they understand what they're
  dealing with
- Storm cleared; they could see the frozen mountain ridge, the valley below,
  the frozen river running to the sea, and a settlement in the distance
- A pride of sabretooth cats attacked as the party descended the glacier —
  one slain, the others driven off
- A frost giant scout was spotted watching from a ridge above, then retreating
  north — his report reaches Torma before the party reaches Farthim

E04: The Settlement
Status: IN PROGRESS — resumes next session

What happened (Session 1):
- Babas flew an aerial survey before the party descended and spotted:
    - The ring — an Estaran artifact marking the old Shield boundary,
      approximately one mile from the town center [edit: exactly one mile from town center, which reflects Estaran precision]. Party immediately
      recognized it as Estaran.
    - The frost giant scout continuing north along the ridge.
    - Something vast moved briefly in the far distance, then vanished back
      into the snow. [DM: this is Gullux; party does not yet know what
      they saw]
- Sylvaris scouted ahead through the abandoned farmsteads surrounding the
  town — stripped of supplies, very quiet. At the town center she [edit: Sylvaris is 'he', an Eladrin Ranger] found
  three humans and a goliath clearing snow from the blizzard, and glimpsed
  a family huddled around a fire inside. No guards, no warriors visible.
  Roughly a third of the town appears deserted.
- Sylvaris withdrew and the party pulled back to plan.
- Session ended here.

Picking up next session:
The party has not yet entered Farthim or made contact. Torma already knows
they are here [ edit: Gullux and some Frost Giants know. Torma does not. She wouldn't be immediately interested. More on her later ]. The giants may probe the village before the party is ready to
move. The village's first question, when contact happens: "Did the Estarans
send you?"

Key things available to discover in town:
- The elders (Gorven, Amalia, Marx) and the history they hold
- The Generator Hall and the Projector Hall — the two intact Estaran structures
- The Projector Hall has wall diagrams pointing to three other Estaran worlds,
  names in Estaran runes that can be copied
