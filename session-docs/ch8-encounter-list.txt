CHAPTER 8: ACROSS THE VOID — ENCOUNTER OUTLINE
===============================================

Terse outline. Each encounter ≤25 words. See game-book/nodes/ for full details.


E01: A Word with Xilo  [COMPLETE]
Farewell on the road to Metgal. Xilo's thoughts on heroism. Last night with Yrk, Gallifax, and Maelric.

E02: The Radiant Lance  [COMPLETE]
Heart of Metgal crater. Felicity aligns the Lance, Cetyri reads the paths, party crosses to Ikellor. Gallifax and Maelric remain.

E03: Shock of Cold  [COMPLETE]
Arrival on the glacier in a blizzard. Sabretooth ambush. Frost giant scout spotted. Settlement glimpsed in the valley below.

E04: The Settlement  [IN PROGRESS]
First contact with Farthim. Shock, then conviction the Estarans sent them. Two perils: food and giants. Estaran halls explored. Star chart: Aerus, Maydor, Ozarrid.

E05: Field Encounter — Frost Giants  [PLANNED]
Eastward foray draws a giant patrol. Giants react with horror — they too believe the Estarans sent the party. They fight to the death.

E05b: The Old Garrison  [PLANNED]
Four sub-areas: basement, great hall, Gullux's rafters, Bronik's control tower. Frost giants and both dragons. Bronik bargains if Gullux falls first.

E06: Torma's Realm  [PLANNED — LATER SESSION]
Through the Dead Forest to the dead Feywild. Torma the Ancient in her lair. Free the hatchling Cetyri. First merge.
